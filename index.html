How it works

1

2

Academic integrity & allowed use

Write4You is a scribing and fair‑copy service. We do not enable ghostwriting or cheating.

Allowed

  • ✓ Converting your own draft into neat fair copy
  • ✓ Redrawing diagrams you specify
  • ✓ Accessibility scribing for students who need it
  • ✓ Sharing personal study notes (not exam answers)

Not allowed

  • × Ghostwriting graded assignments
  • × Plagiarism or policy violations
  • × Selling exam content / answer keys

Orders breaking these rules are rejected and refunded from escrow.
